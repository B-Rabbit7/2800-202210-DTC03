Blockchain Technology

What is Blockchain technology?

Blockchain technology is secure system of immutable transactions across a network of computer systems that are on the blockchain. It's essentially a digital ledger of transactions which is almost impossible to cheat or hack. Everytime a digital transaction occurs, the record of that transaction is saved and imprinted on a block within the chain and the participants' ledgers.
